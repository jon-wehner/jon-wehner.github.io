About Me

I have been interested in software and technology for much of my life. I learned HTML and made my first personal web pages in high school. In college, I majored in construction engineering and pursued a career in the construction industry. I chose this because I enjoyed the challenge of solving complex engineering problems and thought that construction sites were interesting places to work. Upon graduation, I worked as a project administrator for a dredging company. In this role, I was responsible for handling the project documentation for federal and state dredging contracts. I greatly enjoyed the days when I was able to work with the computer systems on our equipment. In August of 2020, I decided that it was time to switch to a career in software development. I enrolled at App Academy, an intensive 6-month coding boot camp where I learned JavaScript and Python. I have created multiple full stack web applications that showcase my skills in React/Redux, Flask, JavaScript, and PostgreSQL.
